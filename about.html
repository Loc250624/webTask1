Core Philosophy

Rooted In Circular Design

Founded in 2024, EcoVerse was born from a simple observation: modern convenience generates permanent waste. We realized that trying to clean up plastic is a losing battle unless we completely re-engineer what we use daily.

We partner exclusively with sustainable certified forestry agencies and zero-waste, carbon-neutral manufacturing facilities. Every single product we design is rigorously evaluated through a lifecycle audit to guarantee it leaves no negative trace.

FAQ

Clarifying Our Commitments

Every product material goes through rigorous laboratory testing to verify decomposition. Our bamboo, cotton, and botanical soaps dissolve within 90 days in home composting setups without releasing any toxins or synthetic compounds.

We partner directly with family-owned farms in Indonesia and Vietnam that practice regenerative polyculture. All botanical oils are cold-pressed, cruelty-free certified, and organically farmed without industrial pesticides.

We audit our logistics partners to compute exact fuel emissions for every courier route. We purchase certified offset tokens that fund native forest conservation, planting and nurturing trees that store equivalent greenhouse gases.
